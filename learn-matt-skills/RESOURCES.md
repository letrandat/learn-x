## Background reading (the "why", cited in lessons)

- Hunt & Thomas, *The Pragmatic Programmer* — small deliberate steps; "no one knows what they want."
- Eric Evans, *Domain-Driven Design* — ubiquitous language (the `CONTEXT.md` idea).
- John Ousterhout, *A Philosophy of Software Design* — deep modules (the `codebase-design` idea).
