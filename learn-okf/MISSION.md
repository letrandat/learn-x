# Mission: Local OKF LLM Wiki

## Why
Build and maintain a personal and professional local LLM wiki structured in the Open Knowledge Format (OKF) to serve as a portable, standardized external memory that local and workspace AI agents can easily query and reason over at home and at work.

## Success looks like
- Design and structure a personal/professional OKF Bundle containing real concept documents (e.g., projects, runbooks, daily logs).
- Learn the strict directory, naming, and frontmatter specs of OKF v0.1.
- Write or run a local script/visualizer to query, link, and display the wiki as a relationship graph.
- Configure a local LLM agent to read and update the OKF wiki pages programmatically.

## Constraints
- Keep it simple, light, and local (pure Markdown, YAML frontmatter, standard folder layouts, and Git).
- Focus on local LLM use cases and personal productivity (no dependency on heavy enterprise cloud platforms).

## Out of scope
- Enterprise metadata catalog platforms (e.g. Dataplex, Collibra) beyond understanding how they relate to the open spec.
